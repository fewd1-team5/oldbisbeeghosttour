- FAQ -

1. Where do we meet for the tour?

Please refer to the confirmation e mail you received when you made the reservation. All the information is on the e mail including what time to arrive, where to go, how long the tour lasts, and what to bring.
Please note that our Ghost Hosts only show up if there is a reservation for their designated tour. If you already know where to meet, but you don't have a reservation, there is no guarantee a tour will be taking place. MAKE A RESERVATION!

2. Is the tour appropriate for my child?

Good question which we can not answer because we do not know your child. It is at the parent's discression to determain whether or not their child is easily scared, and if the tours and hunts are appropriate for them. We recommend if you have young children to take our Bisbee Historic Ghost Walk on Saturdays at 5:30pm, as that is our family friendly tour.

3. Do you guarantee we will see a ghost.

This is the most asked question we get. Lets just say if we could guarantee you would see a ghost, we would charge a lot more money!!

4. How long does the tour last?

The answer to this question depends on which tour you are taking. Please go to the top of our website, where it says 'Our Tours' and from the pull down menu click on the tour you are taking or want to take. In the description it will tell you approximately how long the tours take. Or you can check your confirmation e mail

5. Can I bring my pet with me?

On the Old Bisbee Ghost Tour you are allowed to bring your pet as long as they are well behaved and can be carried or be on a leash for the duration of the tour. We have had dogs, cats and pigs take the Old Bisbee Ghost Tour! We do not recommend bringing them on our Ghost Hunts or Haunted Pub Crawl unless they are a service animal.

6. How long does it take me to get to Bisbee?

We can't answer that because we don't know where you are. We highly recommend downloading the WAZE app on your smart phone. Type in Bisbee and it will tell you how long it will take from you to drive from your current location.

7. Cancellation Policy.

Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours prior to the tour start time. If you fail to do so you will be charged the full amount of the tickets purchased. Cancellations MUST BE MADE by calling 520-432-3308. Cancellations will NOT be accepted via text message or e mail.

8. Local Travel Information.

DiscoverBisbee.com is the city of Bisbee's official Visitor Center's website. All information regarding where to eat, sleep, and play are on this website. It is a great Bisbee, AZ resource! One of our favorite haunted hotel's in Bisbee is the Bisbee Inn/Hotel Lamore. Call them at 520.432.5131 or visit their website to make a reservation. The famous and haunted Copper Queen Hotel is perfect if you want to experience ghosts as well as a comfy bed, pool, and attached bar! Ghost Host Renee loves designing spooktacular goodies for her business, Sweet Midnight. At Sweet Midnight Halloween is everyday and you can get creepy cool clothes, coffin bags and accessories there!
